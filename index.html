About Me

Han Li holds a B.S. in Computer Science and Technology from Jilin University (JLU) and is currently pursuing his studies at the University of Science and Technology of China (USTC). He possesses a wide array of interests in computer systems-related domains, including (but not limited to) software engineering, high-performance computing, and system software. His current research centers on optimizing the performance of Mixture of Experts (MoE). Han Li has previously interned at Ctrip Group as a Software Engineer and later at MetaX through the AI Computing and General Computing Library Talent Program. Driven by an insatiable curiosity, he constantly seeks to expand his knowledge and embrace new challenges.
